About Kandy

Kandy, the majestic hill capital of Sri Lanka, is a UNESCO World Heritage site surrounded by sweeping green mountains and lush tea plantations. As the last royal kingdom of the island, it remains a vital bastion of traditional Sinhalese culture, art, and heritage. The scenic Kandy Lake forms the peaceful heart of the city, reflecting its rich colonial and royal architecture.

Navigating the winding roads of the hill country requires experience and precision. Ride Lanka Cabs provides highly professional private cab services tailored for city tours, corporate trips, and intercity travel. Our expert drivers handle the hilly terrains effortlessly, giving you a smooth and safe ride in our clean, air-conditioned fleet.

Things to Experience in Kandy

Immerse yourself in the rich history and cool climate of Kandy with our dependable transport services.

Sri Dalada Maligawa

The iconic Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic, Sri Lanka’s most revered Buddhist shrine. Located within the royal palace complex, it features stunning classic architecture.

Royal Botanical Gardens

Renowned for its collection of orchids, giant bamboo, and the famous avenue of royal palms. A massive 147-acre garden that dates back to the Kandyan kingdom era.

Kandy Lake (Kiri Muhuda)

An artificial lake built in 1807 by King Sri Wickrama Rajasinghe. It offers a beautiful path for evening strolls right next to the historic cloud wall (Walakulu Bamma).
