About Ella

Ella is a small, peaceful town nestled in the misty highlands of Sri Lanka. Known worldwide for its breathtaking mountain views, sprawling green tea plantations, and cool refreshing climate, it is a paradise for hikers, nature lovers, and travelers seeking tranquility.

Navigating the winding roads of the hill country requires experience. Ride Lanka Cabs provides reliable private transport in Ella with experienced local drivers who know the mountainous terrain perfectly. Whether you are arriving from Colombo, looking for a ride to the south coast, or planning a local sightseeing tour, we ensure a smooth, secure journey.

Things to Experience in Ella

Explore the absolute best highlights of Ella with our comfortable private taxi tours.

Nine Arch Bridge

An architectural marvel of the colonial era. This iconic stone train bridge stands hidden inside dense tropical jungle and lush tea fields.

Little Adam's Peak

An easy, highly rewarding hike that takes you through beautiful tea estates up to a mountain summit with epic 360-degree panoramic views.

Rawana Falls

One of the widest and most majestic waterfalls in Sri Lanka, conveniently located right next to the main Ella-Wellawaya mountain road.
