Privacy Policy

How we protect your personal information and booking data.

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide a smooth booking experience, Ride Lanka Cabs collects essential personal information when you request a ride, including your full name, contact number, email address, pickup/drop-off destinations, and flight numbers (specifically for airport transfers Sri Lanka).

2. How We Use Your Data

The information collected is explicitly used for fulfilling your transport requests, dispatching vehicles via our 24/7 network, maintaining real-time communication with you via SMS or WhatsApp, and improving our customer satisfaction scores across the island.

3. Data Protection & Security

Your privacy is paramount to us. We implement strict digital safeguards to ensure your booking data remains secure. We **never** sell, rent, trade, or leak your personal parameters to third-party marketing companies. Only authorized administrative personnel and your assigned driver will have access to necessary transit specifications.

4. Cookies and Web Analytics

Our website utilizes minor browser cookies to analyze internet traffic patterns and enhance layout performance, ensuring that searching for a "cab service near me" loads our local booking utilities seamlessly on your device.

5. Contacting Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to purge your historical logs from our database, please reach out to our team at ridelankacabs@gmail.com.
