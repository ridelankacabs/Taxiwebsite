About Anuradhapura

Anuradhapura, one of the ancient capitals of Sri Lanka, is world-famous for its well-preserved ruins of a core South Asian civilization. This UNESCO World Heritage site serves as the center of Theravada Buddhism on the island, characterized by majestic brick stupas, ancient monastic complexes, and sprawling man-made reservoirs built thousands of years ago.

Because the ruins and sacred monuments are spread across a vast archaeological zone, touring the ancient city comfortably requires a dedicated private vehicle. Ride Lanka Cabs offers reliable outstation drops, hotel pickups, and customized heritage tours. Our professional drivers ensure a peaceful, seamless journey through this historic sanctuary.

Things to Experience in Anuradhapura

Immerse yourself in centuries of ancient heritage and devotion with our premium transport fleet.

Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi

Pay homage to the sacred fig tree, grown from a cutting of the historical Bodhi tree under which Lord Buddha attained enlightenment. It is the oldest human-planted tree in the world.

Ruwanwelisaya Stupa

An architectural marvel built by King Dutugemunu. This colossal white dome stands as one of the world's tallest ancient monuments, deeply revered by Buddhists worldwide.

Mihintale Peak

Located just 12 km from the main city, this mountain peak is celebrated as the cradle of Buddhism in Sri Lanka, offering pristine shrines and gorgeous sunset views over the plains.
