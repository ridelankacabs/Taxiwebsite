Van Hire Service Overview

Ride Lanka Cabs offers premier luxury and budget van hire services tailored perfectly for group travels, extended family vacations, corporate excursions, and reliable airport passenger pickups across Sri Lanka. We eliminate the frustration of splitting your family or team into multiple smaller cars by offering expansive 2 to 14-seater options.

Our fleet comprises pristine, meticulously inspected vehicles that promise maximum safety and long-distance driving endurance. Every booking includes an exceptionally skilled, background-verified dual-language professional chauffeur who deeply understands tourist destinations and routes, letting your entire group sit back, relax, and create unforgettable travel memories together.
