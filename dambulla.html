About Dambulla

Dambulla, situated in the North Central Province, is a major commercial and tourist hub within Sri Lanka's historic Cultural Triangle. Renowned for harboring the largest and best-preserved cave temple complex on the island, the city acts as the primary gateway to ancient ruins, eco-parks, and heritage fortresses. Its bustling economic center handles the distribution of massive fresh produce nationwide.

Exploring the vast archaeological monuments and national wildlife reserves around Dambulla demands reliable, flexible travel logistics. Ride Lanka Cabs offers professional private cab services, giving you access to experienced drivers and air-conditioned vehicles perfect for cross-country routes, historic city hops, and safe night journeys back to your hotel.

Things to Experience in Dambulla

Journey into the epic spiritual roots and natural reserves of Dambulla with our premium vehicles.

Royal Cave Temple

A breathtaking UNESCO World Heritage site consisting of five massive caves filled with ancient Buddhist murals, intricate ceiling paintings, and over 150 statues.

Golden Buddha Statue

Located right at the entrance of the cave temple hill, this giant, striking golden sculpture stands alongside the Dambulla Museum, showcasing magnificent architecture.

Popham's Arboretum

Sri Lanka's unique dry-zone arboretum, created by Sam Popham. A serene sanctuary featuring a vast collection of native timber trees and peaceful walking trails.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to frequently asked questions about our private taxi services in Dambulla.

How far is Sigiriya Rock Fortress from Dambulla town?

Sigiriya is located roughly 20-25 kilometers away from Dambulla. By private cab, it takes approximately 30 to 40 minutes of pleasant driving.

Can we request a safari drop to Minneriya National Park?

Certainly. We provide drops and round trips to Minneriya, Kaudulla, or Hurulu Eco Park for wild elephant safaris starting directly from your Dambulla hotel.

Is it possible to schedule a midnight pickup from Dambulla?

Yes, our reservation and dispatch systems operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Please ensure you book in advance to confirm your late-night or early-morning ride.
