Top Things to Experience in Kalutara

Don't miss these beautiful destinations during your visit. Let our professional drivers take you there safely.

Kalutara Bodhiya

One of the most sacred Buddhist shrines in Sri Lanka, featuring the world's only hollow Buddhist stupa right by the Kalu Ganga.

Calido Beach

A beautiful, narrow strip of beach that separates the peaceful waters of the Kalu River estuary from the crashing waves of the Indian Ocean.

Richmond Castle

A magnificent two-story Edwardian mansion built in 1910, reflecting striking British-Indian architectural grandeur.
