About Nuwara Eliya

Known as 'Little England', Nuwara Eliya is a picturesque city in the central mountain range of Sri Lanka. Renowned for its cool climate, colonial-era bungalows, and sprawling lush green tea plantations, it stands out as a premier holiday destination.

Ride Lanka Cabs offers premium transportation services throughout Nuwara Eliya. Whether you require a vehicle to navigate winding mountain pathways safely, or are seeking a comfortable transfer to the airport, our certified local drivers are at your service.
