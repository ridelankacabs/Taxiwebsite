About Sigiriya

Sigiriya, dominated by the colossal 200-meter-high ancient rock citadel, is Sri Lanka’s most iconic landmark and a UNESCO World Heritage site. Built by King Kashyapa in the 5th century, this architectural marvel features stunning ancient frescoes, advanced hydraulic pleasure gardens, and the massive carved lion's paws guarding the grand staircase.

Navigating between Sigiriya's historic ruins, nearby eco-villages, and elephant sanctuaries is smoothest with a dedicated private vehicle. Ride Lanka Cabs provides reliable taxi services, outstation drops, and custom day-tours right from your resort. Enjoy air-conditioned luxury and punctual service as you uncover the secrets of the fortress and its lush rural plains.

Things to Experience in Sigiriya

Delve into majestic history and pristine wildlife experiences with our premium fleet.

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Climb the historic palace fortress to witness timeless engineering, advanced water networks, the Mirror Wall, and panoramic views of the vast forest canopy.

Pidurangala Rock

A popular hiking peak adjacent to Sigiriya, widely acclaimed for offering the absolute best views of the Sigiriya Citadel during sunrise or sunset.

Minneriya Wildlife Safari

Located just a short drive away, this sanctuary offers thrilling wildlife safaris to witness the world-famous gathering of hundreds of wild Asian elephants.
