About Jaffna

Jaffna, positioned at the northernmost tip of Sri Lanka, is a vibrant city rich in Tamil culture, fascinating colonial history, and stunning coastal landscapes. Famous for its golden-domed kovils, legendary local cuisine, and untouched tropical islands, Jaffna offers an entirely distinct travel experience altogether away from the typical tourist trails.

Because the top attractions and coastal piers are spread across the northern peninsula, a reliable private vehicle is vital to exploring efficiently. Ride Lanka Cabs offers long-distance drops from Colombo, airport transfers, and expert local sightseeing services. Travel comfortably in our clean, modern vehicles with professional drivers who know the peninsula inside out.

Things to Experience in Jaffna

Discover vibrant architecture and historic island landmarks with our highly reliable cab services.

Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil

Witness the spectacular golden arches and spiritual grandeur of Sri Lanka's most significant Hindu temple complex, renowned for its strict traditions and striking Dravidian architecture.

Jaffna Fort

Explore the expansive star-shaped fortress built by the Portuguese in 1618 and later fortified by the Dutch. It overlooks the scenic Jaffna lagoon and stands as a symbol of colonial history.

Nagadeepa Island

Take a scenic boat ride to Nainativu island to visit this ancient Buddhist sanctuary, revered as one of the places Lord Buddha visited on the island to settle a dispute between Naga kings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick information regarding cab bookings and long-distance transfers to Jaffna.

Can you arrange transport to Kurikadduwan (KKD) jetty for the Nagadeepa ferry?

Yes, we arrange direct transfers from your hotel in Jaffna city straight to the Kurikadduwan jetty, and our driver can wait until you return from the island.

How long is the drive from Colombo International Airport (BIA) to Jaffna?

It typically takes around 6.5 to 7.5 hours via the main intercity highways, depending on traffic conditions and requested rest stops along the way.

Yes, we provide experienced, polite drivers who are highly familiar with northern routes, checkpoints, and local sightseeing landmarks to ensure a smooth trip.
